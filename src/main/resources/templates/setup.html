Setup TeamTactics

Download and install the client software package on your iRacing computer.

It's recommended to install the software just for your own user - not for all users on your computer.

During installation you will need your iRacing ID and your personal
client access token. The following values are saved in your profile:

You may now complete your software setup.

Start the client software (There should be an Icon on your desktop) and go racing. Follow your team members data on the TeamTactics Server.
